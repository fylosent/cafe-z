Privacy Policy for Bryan's Cafe

Bryan's Cafe respects the privacy of customers, students, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when you use this website. This policy has been prepared with reference to the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), so that our information handling practices are explained in a clear and transparent way.

Information We Collect

The main personal information collected through this website is information that you choose to provide through the contact form. This may include your first name, last name, email address, message subject, and the details you include in your message.

We may also collect limited non-personal or technical information when the website is used, such as browser type, device information, approximate usage data, and general website activity. This information helps us understand how the website is performing and where improvements may be needed.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected through the website may be used to respond to enquiries, provide customer support, improve website content, review the quality of our online service, and help maintain the security and reliability of the website.

  • Responding to messages and enquiries submitted through the contact page
  • Reviewing feedback and improving customer communication
  • Monitoring website performance and usability
  • Protecting the website from misuse, spam, or unauthorised activity

Collection, Storage, and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. Personal information submitted through the website is only kept for as long as it is reasonably required for communication, administration, or record keeping connected to the enquiry or service request.

While we aim to apply appropriate security practices, no internet-based system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. For that reason, users should avoid sending sensitive personal or financial information through the website unless specifically requested through a secure method.

Disclosure of Information

Bryan's Cafe does not sell or rent personal information to third parties. Information may only be disclosed where it is reasonably necessary to operate the website or where disclosure is required or authorised by law.

Cookies and Website Analytics

The website may use cookies or similar tools to support basic functionality and understand how visitors interact with the site. You can manage cookie settings through your browser; however, disabling cookies may affect how some parts of the website function.

Access and Correction

Where appropriate, you may request access to personal information we hold about you, or ask for corrections if the information is inaccurate, incomplete, or out of date. Requests can be made through the contact page of this website.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the website, our communication practices, or applicable privacy requirements. The latest version will always be published on this page.

Contact About Privacy

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or about how information is handled through the website, please contact Bryan's Cafe through the Contact page.
