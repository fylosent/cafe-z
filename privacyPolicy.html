Privacy Policy for Bryan's Café


At Bryan's Café, we value the privacy of our customers and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website. By accessing and using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.


Information We Collect


We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when using our website, particularly through the contact form. This information may include your name, email address, and any message or enquiry you submit.

In addition, we may automatically collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing behaviour. This data is collected to improve website performance and user experience.


How We Use Your Information


The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To respond to customer enquiries submitted through the contact form
  • To improve the functionality and performance of our website
  • To analyse user behaviour and enhance user experience
  • To maintain website security and prevent misuse

Data Protection and Security


Bryan's Café takes appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. While we implement standard security practices, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.


Cookies and Tracking Technologies


Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us understand how users interact with our website. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings; however, this may affect certain features of the website.


Third-Party Services


Our website may include links to third-party services such as Google Maps. These external sites have their own privacy policies, and Bryan's Café is not responsible for their content or practices. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites they visit.


Children’s Privacy


Bryan's Café does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that such information has been provided, we will take immediate steps to remove it from our records.


User Rights


Users have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about them and request corrections or deletion where applicable. To make such a request, users can contact us through the details provided on our Contact page.


Changes to This Policy


Bryan's Café may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and users are encouraged to review the policy periodically to stay informed.


Consent


By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
